Case Study 01 · Overview

Universal Loan Customer Onboarding

Customer & Operations Architecture case study exploring how a fragmented lending onboarding process can be redesigned as an end-to-end banking capability.

Architecture Question Back to Case Study
Executive Summary

Redesigning onboarding as a Customer & Operations capability.

This case study explores how a fragmented lending onboarding process can be redesigned as an end-to-end Customer and Operations capability.

The objective was to improve onboarding quality, operational efficiency, banker enablement, compliance readiness, and servicing outcomes by aligning customer journeys, operational workflows, Salesforce capabilities, fulfilment controls, and governance processes into a single target-state architecture.

The solution demonstrates domain architecture thinking across Customer, Operations, Risk & Compliance, Core Banking, and Salesforce enablement domains.

Architecture Question

How do we onboard a customer across channels while maintaining compliance, servicing readiness, and operational efficiency?

The architecture challenge was to connect customer intent, banker activity, fulfilment controls, compliance requirements, platform capability, and servicing readiness into one coherent onboarding capability.

Business Context

Universal Loan was more than a lending journey.

Universal Loan onboarding operated across relationship banking, customer discovery, lending fulfilment, risk checks, document readiness, compliance controls, and servicing handover. The customer experienced one journey, but the bank executed it through multiple teams, processes, systems, and decision points.

Architecture Domains

The domains involved in the solution.

Customer Domain

Customer need, product understanding, onboarding experience, document readiness, and servicing expectations.

Operations Domain

Sales preparation, fulfilment validation, exception handling, ownership, and workflow execution.

Risk & Compliance Domain

KYC/FICA readiness, credit consent, collateral validation, approval controls, and auditability.

Salesforce Enablement Domain

Case management, knowledge articles, guided workflows, activity tracking, document handling, and reporting visibility.

Core Banking Domain

CMS checks, facility limits, security status, fulfilment validation, and account/facility activation dependencies.

Governance Domain

Architecture decisions, product rules, process ownership, quality gates, operational MI, and continuous improvement.

Key Architecture Decisions

Decisions that shaped the solution.

Use Salesforce Case Management

Reuse existing platform capability and reduce implementation cost.

Surface Knowledge in Banker Workflow

Reduce dependency on tribal knowledge and support consistent product guidance.

Introduce Eligibility Guidance Early

Improve fit-for-processing quality before the case progresses too far.

Separate Guidance from Training

Keep operational workflows lightweight while still supporting deeper learning.

Retain Tactical Low-Code Approach

Respond to funding constraints while preserving a path to future scalability.

Universal Loan Customer Journey Map

A journey view showing how a customer moves from lending need to onboarding, fulfilment, approval, and servicing readiness.

01

Customer Need

Customer expresses a lending need or funding requirement through a banker, relationship channel, or banking engagement.

02

Banker Discovery

Banker explores customer needs, product suitability, collateral position, relationship context, and initial eligibility indicators.

03

Eligibility Guidance

Eligibility guidance helps prevent weak submissions by surfacing key rules before the journey moves into fulfilment.

04

Document Request

Customer is guided on supporting documents required for assessment, validation, and fulfilment processing.

05

Case Creation

Salesforce case management becomes the operational anchor for visibility, routing, documentation, and workflow tracking.

06

Sales Preparation

Sales preparation packages the customer request into a fulfilment-ready submission with the required business, credit, and operational information.

07

Fulfilment Validation

Fulfilment validates the submission against operational rules, facility limits, security status, risk checks, and approval requirements.

08

Servicing Readiness

Once approved and activated, the customer journey transitions into ongoing servicing, relationship management, and operational support.
