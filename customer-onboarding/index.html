Case Study 01

Universal Loan Customer Onboarding

Customer and operations architecture case study exploring how a fragmented lending onboarding process can be redesigned into an end-to-end banking capability.

Explore Artefacts Back to Portfolio
Architecture Question

How do we onboard customers across channels while maintaining compliance, servicing readiness, and operational efficiency?

The Universal Loan onboarding journey required alignment across customer discovery, banker enablement, eligibility guidance, fulfilment controls, credit exceptions, compliance requirements, Salesforce case management, and long-term servicing readiness.

Architecture View

What the solution connects

The solution reframes onboarding as a business capability rather than a front-end journey. It connects customer intent, banker activity, Salesforce workflow, document readiness, compliance controls, credit decisions, fulfilment validation, and operational servicing.

Featured Architecture Lens

Customer + Operations + Controls

The architecture shows how customer experience depends on operational clarity: who owns the step, what information is required, which controls apply, what systems validate the request, and how the journey becomes service-ready after fulfilment.
