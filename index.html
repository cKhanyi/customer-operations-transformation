This portfolio focuses on customer and operations architecture, while showing how front-end, mobile, integration, platform, and security technologies support scalable banking capabilities.

Web Technologies

Mobile Channels

Relevant to omnichannel onboarding, servicing journeys, mobile-first customer engagement, and consistent experience design across devices.
